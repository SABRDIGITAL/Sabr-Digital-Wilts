How we work

A simple, done-for-you process.

No long meetings, no complicated tools. We take you from “no website” or “outdated website” to a clean, modern, live site — with a clear process you always understand.

Step 01

Discovery Call

A quick, focused call to understand your business, services and what your new website needs to achieve. No tech talk, no pressure — just clarity.

What this means for you

  • • No long meetings.
  • • We understand your goals from day one.
  • • You walk away knowing exactly what we’re building.

Step 02

Content Intake

You send us the basics — photos, logo (if you have one), services, prices and any websites you like. Even if it’s messy, we clean it up.

What this means for you

  • • You don’t have to write perfect “website copy”.
  • • We organise your information into something clear and professional.
  • • You stay in control of what’s included.

Step 03

Design & Build

We design and build a clean, modern website tailored to your business — with trust-driven layout, clear structure and smooth mobile experience.

What this means for you

  • • Your website looks like it belongs in 2025, not 2010.
  • • Customers can actually understand what you do and how to contact you.
  • • You don’t touch any of the tech — we handle everything.

Step 04

Review & Refine

You review the website, tell us what you like and what you’d change. We refine the design, wording and layout until it feels exactly like your business.

What this means for you

  • • You’re involved without needing to “design” anything yourself.
  • • Small details can be adjusted before launch.
  • • You know what your customers will see before it goes live.

Step 05

Launch & Support

We launch your website, connect your domain and make sure everything works smoothly on mobile and desktop. We’re available for minor tweaks after launch.

What this means for you

  • • Your site goes live with no hassle on your side.
  • • Customers can finally find you, trust you and contact you easily.
  • • You’re not left on your own the moment it launches.
